Modest Mouse

We'll All Float On, Alright...

As long as these guys have been around, Modest Mouse has one of the most innovative sounds in the indie music industry. Isaac Brock is a lyrical genius that uses metaphors to highlight the raw subjects of life and death, relatable human conditions, religious experiences, and the deep meaning of existence. It creates a different depth in music that steadily entertains. Isaac's vocals have high and low pitches, from shouting to deep baritone to a southern drawl. Some listeners describe his voice as a "drunken twang." With Isaac's vocals and the band's multiple rhythms of stringed instruments, Modest Mouse creates a variety of slow and fast paced sounds within their tracklists.
